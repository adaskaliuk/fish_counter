# Problem / Проблема

Current match result view shows too small a slice of the session.
A fisherman cannot clearly understand:

- what happened over time;
- when fish/activity spikes happened;
- which button was pressed, and at what time;
- final result element: weight, count, or both;
- what weather was during the match window;
- how wind, pressure, temperature, and weather changes related to catches;
- whether conditions improved or worsened during competition.

The result should tell the story of the match, not only show final numbers.
